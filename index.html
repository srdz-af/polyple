Controls

  • Orbit: left drag. Zoom: wheel.
  • Shift axes button: click the axis cycle button to shift which dimensions are projected as X/Y/Z.
  • Perspective depth: use the depth button on an extra-axis gizmo to include/exclude that axis from perspective depth.
  • XYZ gizmo: drag the gizmo to move/orbit the view, or tap an endpoint to snap the camera to that axis.
  • Extra-axis gizmos: double click/tap an axis chip to add or remove its gizmo.
  • Axis auto-rotate: use the tiny play button on a gizmo to cycle 1x, 2x, 3x, then stop for that axis plane.
  • Remove from gizmo dock: double click/tap a gizmo to remove it directly.
  • Faceted mode: every object uses the same solid multicolor material with one color per coplanar outer face region.
  • Add object: Shift + A.
  • Transform selected object: G move, R rotate, S scale.
  • Mobile transforms: touch and drag the Move/Rotate/Scale toolbar buttons, then release to confirm.
  • Delete selected object: X.
  • Edit mode: Tab. Select vertex with left click.
  • Undo/Redo: Ctrl/Cmd + Z, Ctrl/Cmd + Y.
  • Close this panel: close button, tap outside, or Esc.

Controls Onboarding Guide

1. Move Through The Scene

Use mouse or touch gestures and the XYZ gizmo to orient your camera quickly.

  • Drag on canvas: orbit around the model.
  • Zoom: mouse wheel or touch pinch.
  • XYZ gizmo: drag it to orbit, or click/tap an endpoint to snap to that axis.

2. Manage Extra Axis Gizmos

Axis chips let you add and remove high-dimensional gizmos and control perspective on visible axes.

  • Axis order row: drag chips to reorder projected axes.
  • Add/remove by chip: double click/tap a chip to toggle its gizmo.
  • Remove by gizmo: double click/tap a gizmo in the dock.
  • Gizmo use: drag a gizmo to rotate through that axis plane.
  • Auto rotate by axis: tap the small play button on a gizmo to cycle 1x, 2x, 3x, then stop.
  • Depth button: tap the depth icon on a gizmo to toggle that axis in perspective depth.

3. Transform Objects

Select an object, then use toolbar transform buttons to move, rotate, or scale.

  • Move / Rotate / Scale buttons: drag after pressing to apply, release to commit.
  • Edit mode: toggle with the pencil button, then select vertices.
  • Dimension stepper: sets dimension for newly created primitives.

4. Quick Actions

Use the quick controls for reference.
